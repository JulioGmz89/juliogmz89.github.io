To improve gameplay clarity, I introduced off-screen indicators for enemies and power-ups, ensuring players can easily locate objectives while freely navigating the arena. Leveraging the existing pool manager, I developed a system that maintains a registry of trackable objects and dynamically calculates their positions to display accurate indicators.
